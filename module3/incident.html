1. Recognize Incident Indicators
System slowdowns, unexpected pop-ups, or encrypted files. Suspicious emails, links, or attachments. Unusual login patterns or device behavior.

2. Immediate Containment
Disconnect compromised devices from networks. Avoid interacting with suspicious emails or files. Preserve logs and evidence for IT/security teams.

3. Follow Reporting Protocols
Notify IT/security immediately using company-approved channels. Provide detailed information, including timestamps, screenshots, and actions taken.

4. Support Incident Response Team
Cooperate fully with investigations. Assist in identifying affected systems or data.

5. Post-Incident Review & Learning
Participate in lessons-learned sessions to improve security practices. Apply recommendations to prevent future incidents.

6. Maintain Awareness
Stay informed about emerging threats. Apply proactive security measures to reduce incident likelihood.
